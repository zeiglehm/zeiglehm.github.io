About Us

Our Company

CRITTR is a company founded on six principles. Each letter represents a core value of our company: Collaboration, Research, Innovation, Technology, Thought, and Respect. Our mission is, and has always been, the furthering of scientific discovery and collaboration. We firmly believe that researchers and students should have the ability to monitor the behaviors of animals and share that data with others.






Collaboration

Collaboration is essential to our company. We are constantly working together to make scientific discoveries easier.

Research

Research remains a core aspect of CRITTR. We are constantly conducting research projects aimed at studying animal behavior.


Innovation

Innovation is at the heart of CRITTR's core ideals. We aim for constant improvement, whether it be technoloical or scientific.

Technology

CRITTR aims to provide customers with the most recent cutting-edge technology. Our animal boxes are top-of-the-notch.


Thought

We value critical thinking, whether it is advice from our partners or construcitve feedback from customers.


Respect

CRITTR values respect, as it is one of our hallmark traits. We respect both our business partners as well as you, the customer.
