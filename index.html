Project Name: Blossom’s Cosplay Portfolio

Project goal or purpose. What are we doing and why? Why now? Creating a portfolio website for Blossom Dang’s cosplay. Portfolio would showcase her work, creating credibility in the cosplay community.

How will you know if this project is successful? What metrics will you track? I would know if this project is successful by whether or not Blossom gets more commissions for her cosplay. Additionally, I might add Google Analytics to her site in order to add user data for it.

What personas or audiences does this project help? Who else does it impact? The personas that this project helps are primarily people in the cosplay community. It impacts Blossom, as well as her cosplay friends and acquaintances.

List all the deliverables for your project then list the related activities you and those involved will do to complete those deliverables: The deliverables for this project would be:

    • User personas • Wireframes • Mockups • User scenarios • Website

I will get these deliverables by sitting down with and discussing the entire process with Blossom. I will consult her throughout the entire process, asking her opinions and input as we go along.

Note any deliverables or activities that are not included in the scope: Photographs, commission work, Blossom’s personal and professional information. Testimonials from friends or colleagues.

What constraints are part of this project? What limits does the project have to happen under? The biggest constraint to this project is time, as well as Blossom’s cooperation and approval. I would need to get all her data files for the site, as well as information for her portfolio.
